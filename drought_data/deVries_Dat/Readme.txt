Filelist

Spc_rare_clean_16S.csv: Rarefied OTU table of 16S sequencing data
Spc-rare_clean_ITS.csv: Rarefied OTU table of ITS sequencing data
Env_sub_clean_16S.csv: metadata for 16S OTU table
Env_sub_clean_ITS.csv: metadata for ITS OTU table
tax_16S.csv: taxonomy for 16S OTU table
tax_ITS.csv: taxonomy for ITS OTU table
Data_final_sampling.csv: datafile for the final sampling


Explanation of variables in Env_sub_clean_16S.csv, Env_sub_clean_ITS.csv, and Data_final_sampling.csv:
Fieldnr - mesocosm identity
Code - unique treatment code
Dominant - dominant species (Anthoxanthum odoratum, Dactylis glomerata, Leontodon Hispidus, Rumex acetosa, or None)
Evenness - evenness treatment (Low, Medium, High)
Drought - drought yes (1) or no (0)
Block - experimental block
Sampling - sampling time (1 - befopre drought, 2 - end of drought, 3 - early recovery, 4 - late recovery)
Ao - biomass of Anthoxanthum odoratum in g dry weight per mesocosm
Dg - biomass of Dactylis glomerata in g dry weight per mesocosm
Lh - biomass of Leontodon Hispidus in g dry weight per mesocosm
Ra - biomass of Rumex acetosa in g dry weight per mesocosm
Moist - soil moisture content (%)
Resp - ecosystem respiration (mg C m-2 h-1)
NEE - net ecosystem exchange (mg C m-2 h-1)
Photo - photosynthesis (mg C m-2 h-1)
CH4 - net methane production (mg C m-2 h-1)
N2O - net N2O production (mg N m-2 h-1)
X16S_ngDNA - 16S gene copies (ng DNA-1)
nirS_ngDNA - nirS gene copies (ng DNA-1)
nirK_ngDNA - nirK gene copies (ng DNA-1)
nosZI_ngDNA - nosZI gene copies (ng DNA-1)
nosZII_ngDNA - nosZII gene copies (ng DNA-1)
AOA_ngDNA - ammonia oxidising archaea gene copies (ng DNA-1)
AOB_ngDNA - ammonia oxidising bacteria gene copies (ng DNA-1)
DOC - dissoved organic C (mg kg-1)
DON - dissolved organic N (mg kg-1)
NH4 - plant available ammonium (mg kg-1)
NO3 - plant availbe nitrate (mg kg-1)
InorgN - plant available inorganic N (mg kg-1)
Ao1 - biomass of Anthoxanthum odoratum in g dry weight per mesocosm at the first harvest (growing season 1)
Dg1 - biomass of Dactylis glomerata in g dry weight per mesocosm at the first harvest (growing season 1)
Lh1 - biomass of Leontodon Hispidus in g dry weight per mesocosm at the first harvest (growing season 1)
Ra1 - biomass of Rumex acetosa in g dry weight per mesocosm at the first harvest (growing season 1)
Total1 - total aboveground biomass in g dry weight per mesocosm at the first harvest (growing season 1)
Ao2 - biomass of Anthoxanthum odoratum in g dry weight per mesocosm at the before drought harvest
Dg2 - biomass of Dactylis glomerata in g dry weight per mesocosm at the before drought harvest
Lh2 - biomass of Leontodon Hispidus in g dry weight per mesocosm at the before drought harvest
Ra2 - biomass of Rumex acetosa in g dry weight per mesocosm at the before drought harvest
Total2 - total aboveground biomass in g dry weight per mesocosm at the before drought harvest
Ao3 - biomass of Anthoxanthum odoratum in g dry weight per mesocosm at the late recovery harvest
Dg3 - biomass of Dactylis glomerata in g dry weight per mesocosm at the late recovery harvest
Lh3 - biomass of Leontodon Hispidus in g dry weight per mesocosm at the late recovery harvest
Ra3 - biomass of Rumex acetosa in g dry weight per mesocosm at the late recovery harvest
Total3 - total aboveground biomass in g dry weight per mesocosm at the late recovery harvest
Vegevenness - plant community evenness
SoiNmgg - total soil N (mg g-1)
SoilCmgg - total soil C (mg g-1)
Diam - average root diameter (mm)
RDMC - root dry matter content (g g-1)
Rootbiom - total root biomass in g dry weight per mesocosm
SRL - specific root length (cm g-1)
RTD - root tissue density (g cm-3)
